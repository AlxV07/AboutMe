About

Hello! I'm Alex, a -year-old studying C.S. + AI.

I have experience in software engineering, algorithms and data structures, and AI (Agentic LRMs + NLP, Distributed Learning, XAI). I have been programming for ~ years in Python, Java, and C++, and am also familiar with Swift, Lisp, and Prolog. Feel free to reach out about anything - I'd be glad to connect!
